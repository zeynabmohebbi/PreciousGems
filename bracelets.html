Caring for Your Jewelry

Keep your jewelry away from moisture, chemicals, and perfumes. After each use, clean them with a soft cloth to remove dirt and oils from the surface of the jewelry. Store jewelry separately in jewelry boxes or pouches to prevent scratches. It is also recommended to have your gold and silver jewelry professionally cleaned periodically to retain their original luster.
